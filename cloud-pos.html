Home 
Description: It's literally a cloud-based point of sale (POS) system. Pricing (Subject to change):
  • Free - Up to 3 users (Owner, Manager, Staff), 1 store/location, can only read the latest 100 receipts.
  • Premium - Pricing: 10$/Month or 100$/Annually, unlimited users, unlimited store locations, can read past and newer receipts.
Why choose us?
  • Small (6) but fast team
  • You can add/remove/edit user membership
  • Track/Manage sales
  • Monitor Inventory from one location to the next
  • Make waitlist system
  • Design/Import your receipt design
  • Works on mobile devices.
  • One license for all.

Requirements for using this POS:

  • A. Internet
  • B. Location*
  • C. Device

The POS is designed to be accessible from any device with an internet connection, but certain features may require location access to provide localized services.

The data that you submit/upload when using this POS will be stored on our database. You may choose to opt-out by providing your credentials to connect to your database instead and your credentials will be safely stored in another database.
